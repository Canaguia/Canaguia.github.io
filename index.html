Projects

Here are post-mortems of some of my most recent projects!

The Garden Short-Film

Was a Lead and helped create animation-ready models and rigs for four main characters, along with environmental set and prop design for "The Garden," a short film produced by Animation Research Labs (ARL).

Plane Panic (Game)

Developed "Plane Panic," an action-packed game where players must work together to fly a plene while avoiding obstacles. The game features intuitive gyroscopic movement mechanics as well as engaging gameplay and visuals! Developed in Unity, this game showcases my skills in game design, graphics programming, and 3D modeling.

VR Fire-Safety Simulation

Developed and delivered a VR-based fire-safety simulation for FireSafe, a product used in both Sweden and Norway. I led the development in Unity, focusing on realistic fire behavior, immersive environments, and interactive simulations for training purposes. This project was crucial in enhancing real-world fire safety awareness and training.
